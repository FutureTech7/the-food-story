About The Food Story

Established in 2015, The Food Story has become synonymous with luxury dining and culinary innovation. Our restaurant combines traditional techniques with modern gastronomy, creating an unforgettable experience for every guest.

With Michelin recognition and numerous accolades, we pride ourselves on sourcing the finest ingredients from around the world and preparing them with meticulous attention to detail.

Chef Marco Rossini

Head Chef & Founder

Chef Marco Rossini brings over 25 years of culinary expertise to The Food Story. Trained under Michelin-starred chefs in Paris and Tokyo, he combines classical French techniques with innovative Asian influences.

His passion for perfection and dedication to sourcing premium ingredients has earned him international recognition and numerous culinary awards.
